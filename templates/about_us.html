Our Journey So Far!

It all started two years ago when one of our friends listed his own villa on Airbnb as an experiment. After one year of positive results, we being childhood friends—decided to join forces. We aimed to host more villas around Pune.

Since then, we've grown from managing just 1 villa to 15 villas in less than a year and many more in the pipeline. Our small experiment has turned into a thriving business!

September, 2023:

Started with 1 villa in Mahabaleshwar

May, 2024:

Added 3 more villas in Mahabaleshwar, 2 in Lonavala, 2 in Bhor and 6 more in Goa

What We Aim For:

  • Quality over Quantity: Target to manage only 5-6 villas in any given area.
  • Regional Focus: Establish ourselves primarily around Maharashtra.
  • Individual Promotion: Marketing each villa rather than promoting the location.
  • Exceptional Guest Experiences: Every getaway is truly exceptional and memorable.
